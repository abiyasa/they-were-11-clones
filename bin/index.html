They were 11 Clones

Control

WASD for movement, mouse for aiming & shooting, Space for cloning, Esc:End game

How to play

  1. Shoot as many enemies as possible & gain energy bonus
  2. Use energy bonus to add a clone of yourself (up to 11 clones at a time!!)
  3. Deposit your clone at the deposit point to gain score
  4. Repeat
