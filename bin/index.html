They were 11 Clones

Control

WASD for movement, mouse for aiming & shooting, Esc:End game

How to play

  1. Shoot as many enemies as possible & get a clone of yourself
  2. Up to 11 clones at a time!
  3. Deposit your clones at the deposit point to gain score
  4. Repeat!
